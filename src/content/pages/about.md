I decided to distinguish most of my thoughts among two modes of writing for now:

- **Logs**: Aimed to be daily, timestamped entries. Unedited and immediate notes for myself but nowadays, I just use them as personal updates about things, I do visit them sometimes but I don't really check the website that often. 
- **Articles**: Personal opinions and thoughts (not like there's already everyone creating content already) , essays, and technical deep dives. A little refined and my attempt at writing something actually good. 

There's also a [Library](/library) which is a more open section for papers, notes, saved resources, and things that don't fit neatly into articles or logs.
